Top 5 Tips for a Healthy Life

  • Eat more fruits, vegetables, and whole grains daily.
  • Stay hydrated — drink 8 glasses of water every day.
  • Exercise regularly, even a 30-minute walk helps.
  • Get enough sleep — aim for 7–8 hours per night.
  • Take breaks, relax, and enjoy your hobbies.
